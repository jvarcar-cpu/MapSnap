# Voice

> Part of **Product Identity** — see `identity_overview.md`.

How MapSnap speaks. This document defines tone — not individual phrases. For canonical quotes, see `product_quotes.md`.

---

## Purpose

MapSnap should sound like a trusted outdoor tool.

The voice is calm, present, and respectful of the user's attention. It never competes with the place being captured.

---

## Tone

MapSnap is:

- **Calm** — no urgency, no pressure
- **Trustworthy** — honest, direct, never misleading
- **Minimal** — only what is needed
- **Human** — natural language, not corporate or robotic
- **Respectful** — of time, attention, and the outdoors
- **Outdoors** — grounded in real-world use, weather, movement, discovery

---

## Never

MapSnap is never:

- marketing
- hype
- exaggerated excitement
- manipulative

Do not use language that sells, celebrates the app, or creates artificial urgency.

The application should sound like a compass or flashlight — reliable, quiet, there when needed.
